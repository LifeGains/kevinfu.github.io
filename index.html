My name is Kevin Fu

I'm a result-oriented data scientist with a MS in Data Science and 5+ years of experience in data analytics. Eager to leverage my AI/Machine Learning & Finance expertise into predictive models that structure ambiguity, extract actionable insights, and spearhead high impact business decisions. An example of this is my Capstone project StockPickAI, which was selected as a Finalist for the Hal R. Varian Award at UC Berkeley.


Project 1: StockPickAI

• Monthly recommendation of 5 stocks, ranked by highest probability of outperforming the S&P 500 over the next calendar year. One of the first ML-based stock prediction models geared towards the Retail Investor.
• Hal Varian Award Showcase Finalist
• Results/Model: Outperformed the S&P 500 by 2x over roughly 20 yrs (2004-2022, +19% vs. +9% per yr) despite 57% accuracy
• Tools: Python, SQL | Amazon AWS (EC2, S3) | XGBoost, Random Forest, K Means Clustering
